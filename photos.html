Then & Now

Over the past six months, I have changed in so many ways! I created this gallery to display how much I have grown! I also want to share with you more about my best friends and my parents!

My Best Friends

My friend, Pooh Bear, is an Australian Silky Yorkshire Terrier and only weighs about 15 pounds! The first day I was brought home, I was smaller than him!! My other friend, Bo, is a red nose pit bull, and weighs about 45 pounds. He’s not that tall in general, but now I am taller than him! Bo and Pooh Bear have grown into liking me. At first, they didn’t really enjoy my presence because I was so playful, but now Bo and I will play and run around in the yard together. But I will say that Pooh Bear is a bit old and grumpy. He doesn’t like play with me, but I am finally accepting that.

Allie and Sam

Allie and Sam are the best! We enjoy spending time together. We love being outdoors and making new memories. I remember when Allie used to hold me and pick me up whenever I would eat acorns and leaves. Now, she can’t even really carry me! I also stopped eating acorns because they made my stomach hurt and honestly, they didn’t even taste good.

Sam is my idol. I look up to him so much! He always tells me what to do and what not to do. Allie is sometimes a pushover and lets me get away with things. I love being told what to do because it helps me learn how to be a good boy. I am so thankful to be apart of this family!
